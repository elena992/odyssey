  # Odyssey
  This is the place where the best journeys begin. It's a modern platform designed for travelers to explore incredible destinations and organize their next getaway.
  Easily search for your dream getaway and save your favorites with a single click to plan your future adventures.

  ## Features
  - Curated Destinations: Browse through a handpicked selection of world-class travel spots.
  - Smart Search: Uncover your next escape or discover hidden gems by filtering by country or city.
  - Personal Bucket List: Save the places you love with a single click and keep your travel dreams organized.
  - Rich Details: Get all the essential information at a glance, from stunning visuals to key travel insights for every destination.

  ## Tech Stack
  - [ ] **Next.js 15:** Core architecture and routing.
  - [ ] **Zustand:** Global state for searching and favorites.
  - [ ] **SCSS:** Custom and responsive styling.
  - [ ] **TypeScript:** Type-safe development.

  ### Roadmap(Coming Soon)

  - Booking System: Direct reservation with a click.
  - User Auth: Personal accounts to sync your favorites.
